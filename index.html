About Spider-Man 2:

The game is based on American comic books about the character of the same name published by Marvel Comics, as well as on other media featuring this hero. It is the third installment of Marvel's Spider-Man game series and the sequel to Spider-Man (2018) and Spider-Man: Miles Morales (2020).According to the plot, the two protagonists of the game, Peter Parker and Miles Morales, will have to fight the supervillains Craven the Hunter, the Sandman and the Lizard, as well as face Venom.

Marvel's Spider-Man 2 is a computer action adventure game developed by Insomniac Games in collaboration with Marvel Games and released by Sony Interactive Entertainment.

SPIDER-MAN Peter Parker

With amazing spider-like abilities, teenage science whiz Peter Parker fights crime and dreams of becoming an Avenger as Spider-Man.

SPIDER-MAN Miles Morales

Emerging from a universe in need of a new Spider-Man, a Brooklyn teen named Miles Morales rose to the challenge. Reluctant at first, he quickly earned the mantle of a Super Hero.

VENOM

Bonding with an unearthly symbiote, Eddie Brock is given amazing powers and an unstable psyche.
